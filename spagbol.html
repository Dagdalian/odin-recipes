Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

The greatest of all Italian pasta-based inventions.

Ingredients

  • 1kg spaghetti
  • 3 large onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 350g mushrooms
  • 300g canned chickpeas
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • Additional stew vegetables to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook the carrots, onions, other vegetables and garlic until softened.
  2. Drain and add canned chickpeas and mushrooms until lightly crisped.
  3. Add minced beef, cooking and stirring consistently until no longer pink.
  4. Prepare and additonal pan and heat the canned tomatoes and paste.
  5. Add the vegetable-meat mixture to the tomato pan and heat on medium-heat until incorporated. Liquid content should remain higher than solids.
  6. Bring to a boil before leaving to simmer for 1-2 hours, adding seasoning in the final 30 minutes.
  7. Prepare the spaghetti in a pot of salted boiling water, cooked to taste.
  8. Drain spaghetti and ladle bolognese over portion. Store the remainder in an airtight container.
